Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, we recommend 3–6 months to ensure availability for your date. Events typically need 2–4 weeks notice. Gift bouquets and smaller arrangements can often be accommodated with 24–48 hours notice, or same-day if you call.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We deliver throughout Rosewood Heights and surrounding areas within a 25-mile radius. Delivery fees range from $10–$25 depending on distance. In-store pickup is always free, and we'll have your arrangement ready at your requested time.

Can I request specific flowers?

Absolutely. We'll do our best to source your requested blooms, though seasonal availability and market conditions may affect options. If a specific flower isn't available, we'll reach out to discuss beautiful alternatives before proceeding.
